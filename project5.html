Excel - Bike Sales Dashboard

The Bike Sales Dashboard is a fun Excel Dashboard report where you can see the sales performance and demographic analysis of age group and etc. You can see here the average income per purchase between Gender (Male and Female),Number of bikes purchase per customer age bracket, and report that shows the count of purchased bike per commute dinstance by customer.

Filters included on this dashboard are the marital status, education attainment of the customer, and the region. Dataset for this dashboard is excel.
